Yoshimatsu Japanese Eatery

MORE INFO↓

Yoshimatsu is a Japansese Eatery in Tucson, AZ. This project was done during my senior year at the University of Arizona. I noticed that their current website hardly had any structure with there current HTML and also had no style or behavior. I decided to take it into my own hands and make a mock up of what could be a more modern and user friendly design for their website. I mocked up the project using a digital toolkit called Sketch. Finally when I was satisfied with the design it was time to build it with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
